Our Courses & Curriculum

Secondary Education (Grades VIII to X)

Our High School curriculum acts as a strong foundation, aligning with the guidelines set by the Kerala State Board of Education (SCERT). The instruction is designed to foster critical thinking, language proficiency, and primary scientific analysis.

  • Standard Malayalam & English medium instruction streams.
  • Comprehensive continuous evaluation (CE) and IT-enabled learning.
  • Integrated projects, arts, and physical education.

Core Subjects

Physics Chemistry Biology Mathematics Social Science Malayalam / Sanskrit / Arabic English Hindi Information Technology

Classes VIII, IX & X

Kerala General Education Department Board

Higher Secondary Education (Grades XI & XII)

PTMHSS offers specialized streams under the Kerala Higher Secondary Directorate. These programs are tailored for students aiming for advanced degree programs in medical, engineering, research, and financial domains.

Science Stream

Focuses on experimental sciences and analytical mathematical systems.

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
Commerce Stream

Deals with financial practices, enterprise structures, and computing.

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Computer Applications

Classes XI & XII

Kerala Directorate of Higher Secondary Education (DHSE)
