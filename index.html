Our History & Vision

Established in 1995, PTM Higher Secondary School (PTMHSS) is an aided co-educational school located in the serene surroundings of Thrikkadeeri Panchayat, Ottapalam, Palakkad District, Kerala.

For over three decades, we have remained true to our founding principle: to deliver value-based education accessible to all sections of society. Combining traditional values with modern learning methods, we prepare students for classes 8 to 12 under the Kerala State Education Board.

We are known for nurturing student potential through active clubs like National Service Scheme (NSS), Scouts & Guides, and various academic divisions.
