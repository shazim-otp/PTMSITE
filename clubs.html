Student Life

Clubs & Co-curricular Programs

We believe in all-round growth. Students are encouraged to enroll in service schemes and talent clubs to develop character and leadership.

Service

National Service Scheme (NSS)

Exclusively for Higher Secondary students, the NSS unit organizes community service programs, organic farming, and public awareness camps.

Service

Scouts & Guides

Developing character, resourcefulness, and outdoor survival skills through scouts camps, hikes, and environmental rescue drills.

Academic

Little KITES (IT Club)

Kerala's award-winning IT initiative, training students in scratch coding, robotics, 3D blender animation, hardware fixes, and web layouts.

Academic

Science & Maths Club

Inspiring scientific thinking through annual science fairs, mathematical puzzles, hands-on lab experiments, and model exhibitions.

Academic

Environment & Social Club

Fosters environmental stewardship through clean campus drives, planting trees, local history surveys, and forestry tours.

Creative

Vidyarangam Kala Sahitya

A club to foster artistic, literary, and oratorical talents in students, preparing them for Sub-district and District level Youth Festivals.

Creative

Vimukthi & Health Club

Conducting campaigns like 'Say No to Drugs', organizing health seminars, sports training, and teaching stress-relief habits.

Service

Junior Red Cross (JRC)

Training students in basic first aid, hygiene practices, and emergency relief operations to foster compassion and empathy.
