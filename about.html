Legacy & Vision

For over three decades, PTMHSS Thrikkadeeri has stood as an educational beacon in the Palakkad district, shaping hundreds of students into successful professionals and responsible citizens.

Established in 1995, the school has expanded significantly from its humble beginnings. As a private aided institution operating under the General Education Department of Kerala, we provide a supportive learning ecosystem for co-educational classrooms across grades 8 to 12.

We emphasize value-based instruction that is both affordable and modern. By introducing ICT capabilities, smart technology, and various service-oriented cadet camps early on, we ensure our students are well-prepared for higher studies and civic leadership.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, holistic instruction combining technology and social values to nurture intelligent, ethical, and compassionate students.

Our Vision

To remain a premier center of learning that transforms rural communities through literacy, gender equity, and leadership opportunities.
