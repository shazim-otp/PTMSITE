Modern Facilities for Modern Minds

Rich Library

A serene space hosting a rich catalog of general books, academic references, magazines, and newspapers to cultivate strong reading habits.

Science & Math Labs

Well-equipped laboratory spaces with specialized equipment for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, allowing students to conduct experiments safely.

Computer Lab & Little KITES

A modern ICT lab equipped with high-speed computers and internet, supporting IT exams and Kerala's prestigious Little KITES computer education program.

Smart Classrooms

Equipped with smart projectors and digital audio-visual screens to deliver animated tutorials, slides, and educational videos for easier learning.

Playground & Sports Field

Ample space for track and field events, football, cricket, and volleyball training to promote healthy competitive spirits and physical fitness.

Eco Utilities & Pure Water

Equipped with advanced RO filter systems for safe drinking water, organic composting units, and student safety surveillance cams.
